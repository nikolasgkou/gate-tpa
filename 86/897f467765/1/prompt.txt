I would like us to take a product first approach in creating Gate TPA, a trusted personal asssistant that places itself in a phonecall between a caller and a call receiver. The call is rerouted to the service through call forwarding, and depending on the situation, different actions are taken. Three layers are active - the first layers detects if any public/crowdourced records exist of the caller id being malicious. The second layer looks at the whitelists/blacklist of the user and decides if it should drop the call, connect it directly to the user, or do something else entirely, like reroute the call to a different person or to the AI agent. The AI agent itself is an advanced feature, it can intergrate with various other elements and synergize to determine the appropriate call of action - for example, if according to the user's calendar, they are in a meeting, the AI agent will converse with the caller and try to determine whether or not it is worth it to interupt the user's meeting or take notes of who called and pass them along or even ask the caller to try again later.
